Glyphses - A collaborative storywriting app

Create stories together with other users, one small paragraph at a time

  • Begin a story with just a couple of sentences (or a whole page if you prefer)
  • A random story will be assigned to you daily, add a couple of sentences to expand it
  • See who contributed to your story and add the pages you like the most

Complete your daily contribution 3 times to get an extra page

Have 3 page accepted to get a new story
